# Third-Party Assets and Dependencies

This artifact contains experiment code, generated outputs, and a small number
of redistributed or regenerated benchmark assets. Third-party materials retain
their original licenses and terms.

## Datasets

### Breast Cancer Wisconsin (Diagnostic)

- Source: UCI Machine Learning Repository, dataset 17.
- DOI: https://doi.org/10.24432/C5DW2B
- UCI page: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/17/breast+cancer+wisconsin+diagnostic
- License: Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0), as
  stated on the UCI dataset page.
- Citation in manuscript: Street, Wolberg, and Mangasarian (1993), plus the UCI
  dataset DOI where appropriate.
- Redistributed files in this artifact: `data/raw/breast_cancer_wisconsin_diagnostic.zip`
  and the extracted WDBC raw files.

### MONK-1

- Source rule: MONK's Problems benchmark described by Thrun et al. (1991).
- This artifact regenerates the complete 432-row Cartesian design from the
  published rule rather than redistributing an original MONK data file.
- Generated files: `data/processed/monks1_full.csv` and derived null-swap
  summaries.

### Synthetic Designs

The make-classification, mixed-DGP, XOR, and SCM datasets are generated by
deterministic scripts in `experiments/`. They are original generated outputs of
this artifact and are covered by the generated-output license stated in
`LICENSE`.
